1. Web Application Penetration Testing

What It Is: Your website, web app, or API is the public face of your company. It's also the #1 target for attackers. We perform a deep-dive test to find flaws in your code and configuration that could lead to a breach.

Why You Need It: A single vulnerability in your website can allow an attacker to steal your entire customer database, deface your site, or use your server to attack others—all of which can destroy your reputation and cost you thousands in fines and lost revenue.

    What We Test For:
  • OWASP Top 10: We test for the 10 most critical web vulnerabilities.
  • Injection Flaws: (SQL, NoSQL, OS Command) Can an attacker steal or corrupt your data?
  • Broken Authentication & Access Control: Can a user log in as someone else or access admin-only pages?
  • Cross-Site Scripting (XSS): Can an attacker run malicious code in your users' browsers?
  • Insecure Deserialization & Business Logic Flaws

    • 2. Network Penetration Testing (Internal & External)

      What It Is: We test your corporate network from two perspectives:

    • External Test: We simulate an attacker on the open internet, probing your firewalls and public-facing servers for any way in.

    • Internal Test: We simulate a rogue employee or an attacker who has already breached one of your workstations. This tests how secure your internal network is.


    Why You Need It: Your network is the backbone of your business. A single misconfigured firewall, weak password, or unpatched server can be the foothold an attacker needs to deploy ransomware, steal sensitive company files, or shut down your operations.

      What We Test For:
  • Misconfigured Firewalls & Open Ports
  • Weak or Default Passwords (Password Spraying/Brute-Forcing)
  • Unpatched & Vulnerable Services
  • Active Directory & Domain Controller Weaknesses
  • Privilege Escalation (Can a regular user become an Admin?)
  • Lateral Movement (If one machine is hacked, can they reach others?)
